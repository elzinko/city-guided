### ideation-basics (v1.0.0) [core]

Basic guidelines for feature ideation and exploration

#### Imperfect Documents Are OK

In Phase A (Exploration):
- Documents can be incomplete
- Questions can remain open
- Details can be vague
- The goal is to surface unknowns, not to have all answers

#### Explore Before Committing

Before moving a feature to `candidate`:
- Explore at least 2 alternative approaches
- Identify the main risks and unknowns
- Document the rationale for the chosen direction

#### Use Timestamp Naming

All features and epics must use timestamp prefixes:
- Format: YYYYMMDDHHMMSS-slug
- Ensures chronological sorting
- Example: 20260117143012-user-authentication

#### Planning.md Is The Source of Truth

The planning.md file is the single source of truth for:
- Feature status
- Relative priority
- Processing order

Never duplicate this information in feature files.
